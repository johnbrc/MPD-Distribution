# Guidance Notes for Translators #

It is hoped that *MPD Resources* will be translated into a number of particularly African languages. The resources have been formulated to be highly accessible for the purpose of translation.

## Burkina Faso ##

It is anticipated that the principal translation will be French, which is one of the national languages of Burkina Faso. Involvement with the people of Burkina Faso represents one of the principal inspirations responsible for the development of *MPD Resources*.
