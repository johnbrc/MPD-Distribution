# Dr John B Clements

![*Discipleship Training, Léo, Burkina Faso, 2008*][image]

[image]: Léo-2008.jpg

John is a missiological educator, presently developing *Maize Plant Discipleship*, a practical, relevant and accessible discipleship resource for use in African contexts, having been a regular visitor to Burkina Faso, in West Africa, over the past decade. He is also currently working to establish *UKCAM*—the [UK Centre for African Mission](http://ukcafricanmission.wordpress.com).
