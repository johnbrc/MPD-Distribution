#	Republication of MPD ResourcesMPD Handbooks have been formulated to facilitate republication — with the following features.##	MMDWritten in MMD — a form of plain text script that can be exported into a variety of formats, including:-	LaTex-	HTML-	PDF-	DOCX##	Creative Commons LicenseIn the countries of continental Africa, MPD Handbooks can be republished, under a free-of-charge Creative Commons license, allowing free-at-source reproduction and publication providing proper attribution is made. ##	Further Information-	[License](license.md)-	More [about](about.md) Maize Plant Discipleship
