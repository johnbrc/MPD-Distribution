# Dr John B Clements![][leo]John is a missiological educator, having received a doctorate from Fuller Theological Seminary School of Intercultural Studies, in 2012.  As well as developing *Maize Plant Discipleship* John is presently working to establish a *UK Centre for African Mission*. -	Vocational profile <http://jbclements.wordpress.com>, -	UK Centre for African Mission <http://ukcafricanmission.wordpress.com>John is married to Sarah; they have three boys and one girl and, for the last twelve years, have lived in a delightful corner of South West Wales, UK.
