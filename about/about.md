# About Maize Plant DiscipleshipMaize Plant Discipleship is-	a practical, relevant and accessible discipleship resource,-	designed in consultation with missional leaders and learners in Burkina Faso, to be appropriate for use in African contexts,-	reliant simply upon facilitators willing to co-ordinate small-group study, reflection and discussion,-	with the goal of facilitating biblical learning that moves continuously outwards, drawing whole communities into patterns of scripturally-based discipleship, in living dialogue with contextual culture.###	PublicationMaize Plant Discipleship is presently being prepared for publication:-	as a series of short, modular, low-cost handbooks,-	incorporating reflective learning and group discussions,-	accessible for translation into other languages.###	FormulationMaize Plant Discipleship is formulated in response to doctoral research that:-	gathered and analysed qualitative data on the perspectives, insights, beliefs and concerns of Burkinab√© leaders and learners;-	explored significant tensions between modern, Western theological education and scripturally-based discipleship praxis;-	resulted in the award of a [Doctorate of Missiology], from Fuller School of Intercultural Studies (Pasadena, CA), in 2012.## Further Information -	[Metaphor](metaphor.md)-	[Guidelines](guidelines.md)-	[Syllabus](syllabus.md)-	[Versioning](versioning.md)-	[Publication](publication.md)-	[Translation](translation.md)-	[Imprint](imprint.md)-	[Copyright](copyright.md)-	[Republication](republication.md)-	[License](license.md)-	[Author](author.md)-	[Website]Return to [README](../README.md)[Website]: http://maizeplantdiscipleship.wordpress.com[Doctorate of Missiology]: http://jbclements.wordpress.com/missiology/dmiss/
