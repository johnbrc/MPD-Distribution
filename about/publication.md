#	MPD Publication (Phases)The project is being published in several phases.##	Pilot project3 MPD handbooks will be published, to promote the MPD project.-	Facilitators' Handbook-	Modules 1 and 4The pilot texts will then be translated into French, following which a pilot republication and training project will be run in Burkina Faso.-	Facilitators' Handbook-	Modules 1 and 4-	*Planned completion date:	end 2014.*##	Complete SyllabusFollowing the pilot project, the entire MPD Syllabus, constituting 16 handbooks, will be published and made available for translation, in three tranches.###	Tranche 1Pilot texts, revised as necessary-	Facilitators' Handbook-	Modules 1 and 4-	*Available for translation:	early 	2015*###	Tranche 2-	Modules 3 and 5 to 9 (7 Handbooks)-	*Planned completion date:	early 	2015*-	*Available for translation:	mid	2015*### 	Tranche 3-	Modules 10 to 16 (7 handbooks)-	*Planned completion date:	end 	2015*-	*Available for translation:	early	2016*## Further Information-	[About](about.md) Maize Plant Discipleship
