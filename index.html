Maize Plant Discipleship

Maize Plant Discipleship is a modular, scripturally-based learning resource. Derived and road-tested in collaboration with African leaders and learners and formulated in response to doctoral research conducted in Burkina Faso, Maize Plant Discipleship is designed to be practical, relevant and accessible for use in African contexts:

  • Resource Handbook
  • 15 modular syllabus handbooks
  • providing 60 scripturally-based studies
  • 240 topics and 800+ questions
  • using accessible language and concepts
  • tailored to small learning groups
  • incorporating reflective discussions
  • suitable for formal and informal study.

The goal of Maize Plant Discipleship is to facilitate learning that moves continuously outwards, drawing whole communities into patterns of scripturally-based discipleship, in living dialogue with contextual culture.

MPD-Distribution is a digital distribution hub, hosted on Github.com. It holds the files that constitute the published resource, Maize Plant Discipleship. These files are primarily for printing and translation purposes.
